A bit about me

My name is Paula Brown. I am a Interaction Design student born, bred and based in Belfast. The heart of Northern Ireland! I also spent mass amount of my childhood in a caravan stationed in a little town called Groomsport, and this is where i fell in love with all the small things that are great about life, including sunsets! I am currently studying Interaction Design full time at Belfast School of Art and stepping towards creating stunning design in reflection of all the small things, that make life great!

Portfolio

These are the projects i have completed in semester 2 of my first year of Intercation Design at Belfast School of Art.

Monogram

My monogram is simple yet sleek combination of my initials, designed in such way to allow me to portray it as both professional and fun depending on what way i want or need to be represented.

Wordmark

My Wordmark is based of the typeface 'rajdhani'. This typeface is the basis of my brand and felt only appropriate to be used as my wordmark. It works perfectly with both my Monogram and my Visual Marque, and once again helps me display the vibe i want to portray.

Visual Marque

My Visual Marque is much more personality driven, i wanted it to reflect more on who i am and what i do. For as long as i can remember i have always loved balloons (weird i know) they are fun, playful and my favourite decoration! When i turned 15 i got a job as a balloon modeller and i havent stopped since. Now i can pretty much make most things out of balloons, this being said i felt balloons was a very appropraite object to symbolise myself. After alot of internal conflict and narrowing down i finally settled for a simplistic style of a hot air balloon.

My Brand

I have created a brand designed to help mimic my personality, it is simplistic yet characterful. The elements within my brand are fulfilled to there best potential with the help of a calm and inviting colour palette aswell as a small amount of unique illustrations. The communication within my brand is casual and friendly but remains professional throughout, this is accounted for with the use of first person narrative.

Infographic

A infographic on the population and staggering statistics of suicide, broken down into the sections of global, Uk, Great Britain and finishing with suicide prevention helpline! The purpose of my infographic is shock people into realising the reality of how serious and common suicide is and hopefully encourage some to help others or themselves before its too late.

Travel App

Want to take selfies like a celebrity? My app, StarSnaps, helps you capture the perfect picture just like a celebrity. I have designed it to locate your location, then to generate all the hottest celebrity selfies taken at that location. Once you've chosen the selfie you want to recreate, (and admired the celebrities custom illustration) you then snap the perfect picture by using a split camera screen. Finally its voting time, you and other users will get to vote on who's picture is better and if you beat the celebrity you will be rewarded like one!
